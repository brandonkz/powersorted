Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate is this calculator?

Very accurate for estimates. We use manufacturer specifications for wattage and official municipality tariffs. Your actual bill may vary based on usage patterns and billing factors (VAT, levies, fixed charges).

Why is my geyser so expensive?

A 3kW geyser running 6 hours/day costs R 600-800/month. It's your biggest power user. Install a timer to heat only 2-3 hours per day and save 50-70%.

What's the cheapest way to heat water?

Solar geyser is cheapest long-term (free sun!). Gas geyser is cheaper to run than electric. Heat pump is 3x more efficient than electric element.

Should I turn my geyser off completely?

No - turning off/on daily causes wear. Use a timer instead: 2 hours morning, 2 hours evening. Or turn down the thermostat to 55-60¬∞C.

Do LED lights really save that much?

Yes! A 60W incandescent costs R 18/month if on 8 hours/day. A 10W LED costs R 3/month for the same light. Replace 10 bulbs = R 150/month savings.

How can I reduce aircon costs?

Set to 23-24¬∞C (not 18¬∞C), clean filters monthly, close doors/curtains, service annually. Every degree colder = 10% more cost. Consider ceiling fans first.

What's phantom load?

Power used by devices on standby (TVs, decoders, chargers). Can be 5-10% of your bill (R 50-150/month). Unplug or use power strips you can switch off.

Is solar worth it?

Yes, especially with rising tariffs. Solar panels: 5-7 year payback. Solar geyser: 3-5 year payback. After that, it's pure savings. Plus load shedding protection.
